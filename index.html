Unilock is a trading card game focused on how players interact with cards and the gamefield. Its core gameplay revolves around two mechanics: pointing arrows and field rotation.

Unilock cards depict the characters, events, and lore of a profound fantasy space opera, set within a newborn galaxy as it grows and evolves.

In Unilock, players choose unique entities and spells from various planets and archetypes to combine into a single deck, allowing them to forge their strongest alliance.
